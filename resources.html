The Importance of Climate Change

Climate Change is extremely important and relevant today. It is changing and destorying the world we live in. In 2021, the C02 emission increased 6%. This results in catastrophic events, such as increased droughts, large scale disasters, and extreme weather.

What can you do?

There are many individual actions that you can take to help Climate Change. These can be small things, such as using more public transportation, and throwing away less food, to bigger actions, such as changing your energy source and speaking up.

Climate Clock

The Climate Clock is a timer that count downs the amount of time our world needs to get and stay under 1.5 degree celsius increase before the worst effects of Climate Change becomes irreversible. There is only about 7 years left on the clock. We need to start now or else it will be too late.
