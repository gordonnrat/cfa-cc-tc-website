Climate Change

About Climate Change

Climate Change is the changing of the world's temperature and weather. This is caused by the burning of fossil fuel. This increases the carbon emission and leads to higher global temperature. Climate Climate has many effects on our world, including sea level rise, droughts, and more...

Turnabout Choice

About Turnabout Choice

Welcome to my game, Turnabout Choice! This game teaches you about Climate Change in a fun and interesting way. You are trying to save the Earth from Climate Change by catching heat waves that are heating the Earth. Collect 150 heat waves to save the world!

Directions

To move your character, use your left and right arrow keys! Press the spacebar to continue the dialouge. Left and right to select choice. Click on your choice during the riddle!
